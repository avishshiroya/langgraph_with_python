Build Your Own Hauling Network

With myTruckBoss, you're not just digitizing tickets—you're building a customized, connected network of everyone involved in your hauling operations, both inside and outside your organization.

Your network can include:

  • Drivers –Solo or fleet drivers logging and submitting digital tickets in real time
  • Haulers/Brokers –Managing demand, billing, and overseeing operations
  • Contractors –Approving tickets and viewing job progress

Everyone operates from a shared source of truth—digitally.
